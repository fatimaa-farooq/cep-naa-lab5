#Tomato Bruschetta Recipe
## Ingredients:
- 1. Tomatoes
- 2. Bread
- 3. Garlic
- 4. Olive oil
- 5. Salt and Pepper
- 6. Basil
- 7. Cheese
## Instructions:
1. Season your tomatoes with salt, pepper and a drizzle of olive oil.
2. Toast your bread and rub it with garlic.
3. Place your tomato toppings on the bread and add fresh mozzarella.
